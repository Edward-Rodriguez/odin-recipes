Turkey Wrap Recipe

Homepage

Description

Healthy and delicious gluten free turkey wraps!

Ingredients

  • 6 Carb Balance Soft Taco Flour Tortillas, warmed and lightly toasted
  • 1 lb. Turkey breast, ground
  • 1 tbsp. Olive oil
  • 2 tsp. Greek gyro seasoning
  • ¼ cup Cucumber, diced
  • ¼ cup Red onion, diced
  • ¼ cup Pinto beans, prepared
  • 1 cup Lettuce, shredded
  • ¼ cup Tzatziki sauce, prepared
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. In a sauté pan, over medium-high heat, heat the olive oil. Add the ground turkey and Greek seasoning. Cook until browned and cooked through. Season with salt to taste.
  2. Warm the tortilla wraps. Place ½ cup of cooked, seasoned turkey into each tortilla wrap. Top the turkey with 1 tbsp. diced cucumber, 1 tbsp. diced red onion, 1 tbsp. pinto beans, ¼ cup of shredded lettuce, and 1 tbsp. tzatziki sauce.
  3. Roll into a wrap and enjoy!
