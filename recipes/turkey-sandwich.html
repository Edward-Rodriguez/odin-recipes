Turkey Avocado Sandwich Recipe

Homepage

Description

Grilled Turkey Avocado Sandwiches! An easy, healthy and flavorful way to switch up your lunch routine!

Ingredients

  • 4 slices 100% whole grain/wheat bread
  • 1 Tablespoon of Primal Kitchen mayonnaise
  • 6 ounces sliced turkey breast
  • 1/2 large avocado, sliced
  • 1 ounce sliced red onion
  • 2 to 4 tomato slices
  • 2 slices of CHAO cheese
  • 1 Romain Lettuce

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet to medium heat.
  2. Lightly toast the slices of bread.
  3. Flip the bread over and spread 1/2 Tablespoon Primal Kitchen mayonnaise on each slice.
  4. Between two of the slices of bread, divide evenly and place on top the turkey, avocado, tomato and red onion.
  5. Top with 1 slice CHAO cheese each.
  6. Cook 2-3 minutes or until golden brown on the bottom.
  7. Carefully flip over the sandwiches and cook an additional 2-3 until golden brown on the opposite side.
  8. Remove from heat, cut each sandwich in half and enjoy!
